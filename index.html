The Universal Basic Needs Income (UBNI) for the working class.

This debit card comes with a monthly recurring $1000 which is designed specifically for food and water purchases only. Wielding this card halves 50% off unexpected trips to urgent care, 10% medications when swiped at checkout, 25% off rent in qualified living homes.

Eligibility Requirements:

  1. 1. You reside in the City of Los Angeles
  2. 2.You are 18 years of age or older.
  3. 3.Must have a history with proof of working 30 to 40 hours workdays in the last 2 years consistently and or have at least 1 year of 30 to 40-hour consistent work.
  4. 4.Must have an income of at least $13,000 to $55,000 yearly(poverty level incomes) or a combined income with a married partner under the same household.
  5. 5.Must have no drug or alcohol-related addiction/abuse problems.
  6. 6.Must have a clean record within the last 2 years which means: -no more dui's -not have been jailed or in prison. -or have done any other crime.
  7. 7.Must not be in any terrorrist, gang, extremists, groups.

EXCEPTIONS: IF YOU MEET ALL THE REQUIREMENTS ABOVE BUT HAVE GONE OUT OF WORK RECENTLY DUE TO INJURY AT WORK OR “CHRONIC” MAJOR ILLNESS THAT PREVENTS YOU TO WORK. You qualify but have documents of proof of said illness or injury ready to present,

    • About The UBNI Program

    The [Universal Basic Needs Income] program is derived from the concepts of UBI Universal Basic Income. This Card will provide $1000 monthly for 12 months to people at the federal poverty level. Which people can reapply easily by updating the system with their current work wages. It is designed to combat the naive thresholds of the programs like food stamps(EBT) and is meant to raise the baseline of the poverty level to a more hopeful line. After a certain limit the user loses their benefits which can stockpile on their already thin margins of savings and monthly bills to pay. High cost of living, Unexpected Car Maintenance, insufficient wages. On top of that the virus has completely raised the economic difficulties higher than before. With business being closed down or half opened, work hours decreased. Combined with the fact the owning a home seems like an actual dream now. The savings vault is becoming impossibly to fill.

    Why UBNI instead of UBI?

    UBNI is a lot more functional than UBI because it mind you while it does have a certain threshold and the individual must meet there is a reason for that. If people with problems say with severe drugs,alcohol, smoking problems gets ahold of $1000? If people with gang/extremists/terrorist gets ahold of $1000? and then comes the BASIC NEEDS. Food Water and Shelter. Medications. Are the essentials. Of course UBI might reduce bureaucratic processes. But UBNI doesn’t have to be another horribly made government website with long lines to wait or poor customer service. it can be up to date with technology making the processes much less of a headache. When you hire the right person for the job.

    FAQS/Questions Asked

      What happens if I passed the threshold of $55,000 while on UBNI in the middle of the year?

    • Don’t worry You can keep the incoming monthly UBNI until the following January 1st. In which you can reapply if your wages stagnate for whatever reason (left job to find another job or to educate) or cause of (serious illness[need doctors diagnosis]

      • Do you collect data?

    • Yes we do this to determine where the card is being used the most for statistical reasons like where are people being hit the most financially. and how we can improve the user experience of the website.

      • What happens if I get arrested for crime or deliquency or for a DUI?

    • You get 1 warning on email and if you get into trouble with the law again you get suspended from the program and no UBNI will be sent to your card the following month. You can reapply the following year.

      • What do you mean by qualified ‘house spaces’?

    • Houses or apartments that are appropriately needed will be qualified for the halve effect of 25% to take effect. 1 living room 1 bathroom 1 kitchen and must not be more than $2100 ←—- (this can change) as apartment rent can increase dramatically.

      • I have figured out a loophole and misused my card what’s going to happen to me?

    • Immediately after discovery of misuse. YOUR UBNI CARD WILL BE SUSPENDED and you’re BANNED FROM THE PROGRAM (can never reapply again).

      • What happens if I lose my card?

    • You can lock the card number [link here] in your phone by the web app (so you can try to find it) or order a new card [link here]which will delete and make the lost card invalid to use

      • Where Can I use the card to buy food?

    • You can use it anywhere that sells food. Their are no limits in where you can buy the food with this card. its debit card for food.
